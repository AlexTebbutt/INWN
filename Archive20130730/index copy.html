How are projects managed by you?

We have out own approach that follows a phased delivery and feature driven milestones. We firmly believe in involving you as regularly as possible in order to give you what you actually want rather than what we think you want.

Can I see how you're getting on with my project?

We believe in transparency from the get go. We provide a 'Work in Progress' area that you can access and give you regular updates.

Can I make changes to the requirements during the project?

Of course. We understand that as a project progresses, things that you thought may have been right at the outset, don't quite cut it now you've had more time to think on it. Note that big changes may affect budget or schedule.
